# Digital Literacy Project

A comprehensive second-semester project designed to develop essential digital skills, online presence management, professional communication, and cybersecurity awareness.

---

## 📚 Project Overview

This project equips students with practical knowledge and hands-on experience in five critical areas of digital literacy:

1. **Digital Presentations** - Creating and delivering professional visual content
2. **Professional Portfolio Development** - Building an online presence on industry platforms
3. **Online Platform Engagement** - Actively participating in digital learning communities
4. **Professional Communication & Social Media** - Mastering email etiquette and responsible social media use
5. **Cybersecurity & Crime Prevention** - Understanding digital threats and protection strategies

- Professional formatting and structure
- Appropriate tone and language
- Context-specific communication standards

**Social Media Responsibility:**
- Privacy protection strategies
- Information verification before sharing
- Anti-cyberbullying practices

### Task 5: Cybersecurity & Crime Prevention
**Case Study: "The Anonymous Confessions Burn Page"**
- How cyber-harassment campaigns develop and escalate

**Prevention Checklist Includes:**
- Multi-factor authentication setup
- App permission management
- Public WiFi safety protocols
- Identity protection (Masked Aadhaar)
- UPI and financial transaction safety
- Cybercrime reporting procedures in India
