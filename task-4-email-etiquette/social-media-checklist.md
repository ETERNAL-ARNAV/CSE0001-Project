## Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (College Students)

### ✅ Do’s
- Do think before posting anything online.
- Do protect your privacy by adjusting account settings.
- Do verify information before sharing it.
- Do maintain respectful and positive communication.
- Do use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

---

### ❌ Don’ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly.
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online harassment.
- Don’t spread fake news or unverified content.
- Don’t overshare your location or daily routine.
- Don’t use weak passwords or reuse the same password across platforms.
